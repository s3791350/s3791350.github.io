Project Idea - Celebrity Shazam

Overview

- This project is an application for mobile phones which is much like the popular app shazam which allows you to find out what music is playing at the current time except that this app allows you to find out which celebrity is which. By using this app you can put the camera on a celebrities face and the app will tell you who that celebrity is, have you ever wondered who a celebrity is and couldn’t find them? Well look no further because this app is going to change people’s lives.

Motivation

- This project will be useful to people who want to find out who a certain celebrity is. I find myself to be doing this all the time when I see a celebrity in a photo on or video on social media or on the news, this is something that I have wanted to create for a long time, using this new technology it can make it a lot easier for people, you will not need to search google for many minutes or even hours just to find someone. This is an interesting project which can be used by all people of all ages.

Description

- The product will allow users to find out what celebrity is what by going onto their phone and using an app which will then open the camera app and users can point the camera at a photo of the celebrity or the video on tv and the app will tell you who the celebrity is, showing a lot of information on that celebrity as well. The app will tell you once a celebrity is scanned and then will give you information such as what music/movies they have been in, other celebrities who are relevant to that person, different facts will also be shown including their age, height and full name. When a user opens the app with only one click of a button they can get to the camera and start scanning celebrity faces, this will give the user ease of access and will make them come back more as they may think of using the app at times but thinking that it will take too long to scan, this app scans both fast and accurately to make sure the user and the app get the best results possible. The app should have a constant colour such as the app shazam has the colour blue, it should have the colour red or something along those lines and show prominence towards the big words like “scan” so that people will know where to press to do certain things rather than searching for a long time to do something. The app will have a history section so you can see what celebrities or people you have searched for in the past in case you have forgotten their name or what they look like. Links will be given to movies or music that the celebrity is in so that you can listen or watch straight away for example if a person is a singer there will be a link to their Spotify or Apple Music and if they are an actor or actress there will be a link to their imdb page or if their movies are available on Netflix or Stan there will be a link to that. There will be multiple tabs with one of the tabs saying something like “Overview” giving the user a summary of what they have used the app for, another tab saying “search” which is where they will search for the celebrity and one more tab saying “discover” this part of the app shows users celebrities that are related to the people they have searched for example they may be in a song or movie together. The app opens up in the search tab so that users can open it and search straight away. There will also be a support page where users can ask for help and they can also suggest new celebrities who maybe aren’t as well-known who can be put on the app if they aren’t already on it. This app will be both effective and efficient and can be useful for a lot of people including myself as I would want something like this app in my life to make things a lot easier for me.

Tools and Technology

- The software is an app on both IPhone and Android so anyone can use it at any time, as long as your phone has a camera the app will work on it. The app will need to be coded in different languages to suit both devices, as android and apple work on different codes. The code for the apple devices will need to be done in the swift programming language and the code for the android devices will need to be done in a coding language such as Java.

Skills required

- The skills required for the project are things like knowledge of coding languages but there will be a team behind it with the skills needed to create the software. The code will need to be written to make the app run and it may be hard to code but the end result will be all worth it. All people involved will have to know how to code or be a part of the marketing team which you would then need those kinds of skills, in all projects there are different people with different skills who each do their part to make the project work.

Outcome

- If the project is successful the outcome will be that people get an app where they can take a photo of what celebrity is what if they do not know who it is, this app will help them. The original problem is solved as people from all around the world can now find out who a celebrity is just by taking a photo of them or showing the camera to the app for it to recognize who that is. The impact that this will have is that people will no longer be searching for hours and hours to find out who someone is and will simply just go to the app and search for the person that they want without wasting hours of their time just to find someone.
