Interest in IT

What is my interest in IT? When did my interest in IT start? Was there a particular event or person that sparked my interest? IT experience?

- I have been interested in IT ever since I was 3 years old when my brother got a PlayStation 2 for Christmas, I loved watching him play it and eventually I started playing it too. IT is my whole life and everything that I love to do is on a computer or on a gaming console. I gained inspiration from people such as Bill Gates and Mark Zuckerberg as they are some of the most inspirational people in the world, seeing them make these applications and run these companies has inspired me to do the same. The only IT experience that I have is that I used to somewhat build computers a few years ago.

Why did I choose to come to RMIT?

- I chose to come to RMIT because from what I have seen and heard from other people it is a really great university for IT, the location of the university is very convenient for me as it is just 2 trains to get to the university. The entirety of RMIT from the education to the people who go there makes me feel at home and makes me feel like I belong as there are all different races, sex and religion within the university.

What do I expect to learn during my studies?

- I expect to learn about IT in all different areas of IT as IT is in everything, I want to learn about everything that you can do with IT and everything that comes with IT as an industry. I hope to learn about how to get a job in this field and what working in IT is like through group projects/assignments and individual assignments too as in the industry you may need to do both group work and individual work to get the job done
